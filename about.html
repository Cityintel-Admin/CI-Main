Our Mission

CityIntel’s mission is to enhance public and private situational awareness by delivering timely, data-driven insights on protests, demonstrations, and civic events across the globe. We aim to provide stakeholders with accurate, actionable information to support informed decision-making, operational readiness, and community safety.

What We Do

CityIntel aggregates and analyzes open-source and partner-provided event data to identify emerging protest activity, forecast potential disruptions, and monitor associated risks. Using advanced filtering and contextual analysis, our platform provides a unified view of global protest trends — enabling real-time visibility into civic movements and regional patterns.

  • Live aggregation of protest and civic event data
  • Geospatial heatmaps and risk segmentation by severity
  • Predictive analytics for event escalation likelihood
  • Dynamic dashboards for operational monitoring

Disclaimer & Data Sources

CityIntel aggregates data from open-source intelligence, verified media outlets, and selected partner feeds. While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, the information provided is for informational and analytical purposes only and should not be used as the sole basis for operational or policy decisions. Data updates are subject to availability and verification cycles.
