1. What are cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. CityIntel also uses similar browser storage technologies, such as local storage, which work in a similar way but are not always classed as “cookies” — we treat them the same way for the purposes of this policy.

2. How CityIntel uses cookies and local storage

CityIntel is a platform that you log in to use. We use cookies and local storage primarily to keep you signed in, remember your preferences (such as your default map region or dashboard layout), and keep the platform secure. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites, and we do not sell any information collected via cookies.

3. Types we use

Strictly necessary

Used to keep you logged in, maintain your session, and protect the security of your account. The platform cannot function without these, and they do not require consent under UK law.

Preference / functional

Remember choices you make while using the platform — for example, your default map focus region, pinned radius settings on maps, or briefing preferences — so you don’t need to set them again each visit. These are stored using your browser’s local storage and stay on your device.

Analytics

CityIntel does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising cookies. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and, where required, we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential analytics cookies.

4. Third-party cookies

Some pages embed interactive maps using OpenStreetMap and CARTO map tiles. Loading these maps may involve requests to those providers’ servers, which could set their own cookies in accordance with their respective policies. Payment processing for subscriptions is handled by Stripe, which may set its own cookies during checkout in accordance with the Stripe Cookies Policy.

5. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Because CityIntel relies on strictly necessary cookies to keep you logged in, blocking these will prevent the platform from working correctly. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

6. Contact us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please get in touch via our Support page. You may also wish to read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
